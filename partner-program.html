Frequently Asked Questions

When do I receive my commission? +
Commissions are paid out after your referral signs a contract and completes their first payment. Depending on your tier, payouts are processed monthly or instantly.
How are referrals tracked? +
Each partner receives a unique referral link with tracking built-in. You'll have access to a dashboard where you can monitor clicks, conversions, and earnings in real-time.
Is there a limit to how much I can earn? +
No limits whatsoever. The more qualified referrals you send, the more you earn. Plus, as you refer more clients, you unlock higher commission tiers.
What happens if my referral doesn't convert? +
No worries. You're only compensated for successful conversions, but we work hard to close every qualified lead you send our way. Our sales team will keep you updated throughout the process.
