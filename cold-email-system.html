How Our Cold Email Infrastructure Works

With Google and Outlook cracking down on cold email sending, hitting the main inbox and avoiding spam filters is trickier than ever before. When you use Google or Outlook to set up your domains — which 99% of cold email providers do — they are not on your side.

You need to “fly under the radar” with your cold email campaigns, otherwise your domains will get flagged and your emails will bounce or land in the spam folder.

Plus, there’s no cold email support from Google or Outlook, because once again — they don’t want you sending cold emails.

We are on your side. With our private cold email infrastructure, you’ll get hands-on support, troubleshooting assistance, and proactive monitoring to make sure your domains are running smoothly and you’re staying out of the spam folder.

Plus, our sending infrastructure is invite-only, only available to our DFY customers. You won’t be sharing IPs with the masses like you will on Google or Outlook — our IPs are pristine, warmed up for maximum inbox placement, and constantly monitored to ensure continued health.

If you’re not a technical person, let us put it in simple terms: Google and Outlook are like a public golf course that doesn’t want you there — our private infrastructure is an exclusive, invite-only country club with the best customer service on the planet.

Can I Connect This to my CRM?

Yes! Although we do not have a direct integration with most CRMs, we do have an integration with Zapier which allows us to push positive replies and more into CRMs like HubSpot, Pipedrive, Salesforce, etc. This is also something our team is willing to assist with in the onboarding process!

Why do I Need a Cold Email Infrastructure like This?

A cold email infrastructure is necessary because it follows lead generation best practices (high volume, high deliverability, targeted messaging) while remaining compliant. Tools like Gmail, HubSpot, or similar platforms are not built for cold outreach and may put your main domain at risk.

How Long Does the Setup Take?

On average, setup takes around two weeks from signup to launch. Timing depends on onboarding speed and responsiveness.

Will Cold Email Work for me?

As long as your target market checks their email, cold email can work — especially at scale. Our team has worked with 1000+ B2B companies and has seen success across many industries.

Why do I Need to Purchase Domains?

Purchasing separate domains protects your main domain from risk. We recommend sending small volumes from each domain to maintain deliverability and quickly replace any domain if needed. This significantly minimizes risk.
